The rare official german FA (DFB) pocket programme issues

not many collectors know these little and very rare official DFB programmes. The german FA is publishing own programmes for either home and away games of the national squad. I was told that programmes where already issued in the fifties, but the first issue of that kind of football programme is not known. My oldest piece is from 1962. The programmes Cover the A-Team and the Under 21 squad.
The programme is often for two or three games when dates are to close for separate items. Only available for the FA officials, the media, VIP`S and the honoric members of a special DFB supporters club, makes these programmes very rare and sought after.
The big tournament issues are the progs with the best informations. Covering single pictures from all players and staff members beside all the regular information you find in the normal full international issues. Both line ups, and a complete timetable from arriving until departure of the both squads are also included as adresses and telefonenumbers from people being involved with organisation of the match.
